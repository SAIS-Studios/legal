Common questions

How do I start over or clear my data?

Open the You tab, go to Settings, and tap Start over. That resets your profile and clears your saved entries. Because everything lives only on your device, deleting the app removes all of it completely.

Does Meridia collect any of my information?

No. Meridia has no account and no server. Your birth details, journal, and moods stay on your iPhone and are never collected, sold, or shared. See the privacy policy for the details.

Why does it ask for my location?

Only if you use the weather feature. Meridia reads your location once to fetch your local weather, and the coordinates are not stored. Declining location leaves the rest of the app fully working.

My morning reminder isn't arriving.

Make sure notifications are allowed for Meridia in iOS Settings → Notifications → Meridia, then re-toggle the Morning reminder in the app's Settings.

Is any of this real advice?

Meridia's astrology, tarot, numerology, and frequencies are for personal reflection and entertainment. Nothing here is a prediction, and it is not medical, psychological, or financial advice.
