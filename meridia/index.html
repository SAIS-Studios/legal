Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Meridia is private by design. There is no account and no server. Everything you enter stays on your iPhone. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data, and the app contains no advertising and no third-party tracking.

What Meridia stores, and where

To create your readings, Meridia keeps the following on your device only, inside the app's own storage:

  • Your name and birth details (date, time, and place) to calculate your chart and numerology.
  • Your journal entries, moods, intentions, dream notes, and synchronicities.
  • Your daily readings, streaks, and app settings.

This information never leaves your phone and is never sent to us. The developer has no ability to see it.

Location

If you use the weather attunement feature, Meridia asks for your location once to read your local conditions. Your approximate coordinates are sent to the free weather service Open-Meteo solely to retrieve current weather, and are not stored by Meridia. If you decline location access, the rest of the app works normally.

Notifications

If you turn on the morning reminder, Meridia schedules a local notification on your device at the time you choose. This is handled entirely by iOS on your phone. No reminder data is sent anywhere.

Analytics, advertising, and tracking

None. Meridia includes no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no tracking technologies. We do not build a profile of you and we do not use the App Tracking Transparency framework because there is nothing to track.

Third-party services

The only external service Meridia contacts is Open-Meteo, and only when you use weather. No personal identifiers are sent, only the coordinates needed to return a forecast.

Deleting your data

Your data lives only on your device. You can erase all of it at any time by using "Start over" in the app's settings, or by deleting the app from your iPhone. Because nothing is stored on a server, deletion is immediate and complete.

Children

Meridia is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

For reflection, not advice

Meridia's astrology, tarot, numerology, and frequency content is offered for personal reflection and entertainment. It is not medical, psychological, financial, or professional advice, and it is not a substitute for care from a qualified professional.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
